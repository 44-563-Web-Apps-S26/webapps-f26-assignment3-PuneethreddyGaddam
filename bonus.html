Powerful Gorilla and Parker

Team Description

Powerful Gorilla is a strong and fearless superhero who protects the city using his incredible strength and quick instincts. His sidekick, Parker, is smart and creative and helps by making good plans and using useful gadgets. Together, they fight villains and keep people safe.
